# MTO Rules

Use this page to capture project material take-off assumptions and reporting rules.

## Starter Rules

- Count `WELD`, `FLANGE`, `GASKET`, `VALVE`, and `INSTRUMENT` records separately in material-facing reports.
- Keep spool indicators such as `PSPOOL`, `PPLIST`, and `PSLIST` visible in MTO readiness dashboards.
- Treat missing `SPRE` or `PSPE` references as a material traceability warning.
- Export issue registers to CSV so engineering teams can review them outside Obsidian.
